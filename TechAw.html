Tech Awareness Session : Using WhatsApp

About Workshop:

For this 1st workshop, the focus is on creating awareness amongst the senior citizens of the need to change and adopt new technology to keep them connected. The focus for the first session is on using WhatsApp to communicate as well as highlight features available in the phone app.


Topics Covered:

  • Smart Nation Initiative
  • The world is evolving
  • Using WhatsApp for messaging/ making Voice Calls/ making Video Calls
  • Creating Group Chats in WhatsApp
  • Sharing documents, videos and images
  • Bookmarking items on WhatsApp
  • Broadcasting messages
  • Replying to specific messages
    •

    About the Speaker:

    Peter is passionate about training in particular for senior citizens which is a segment in society that is generally fearful about technology. He has also conducted training for corporate clients in the areas of IT skills, leadership and people management.
